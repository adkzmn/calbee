How it works?

Establish a connection between two calendars to duplicate your available and busy time periods across both calendars. Additionally, you have the option to configure a two-way synchronization, enabling all events to appear on both calendars simultaneously.

Also you can hide the specific information of any field within the duplicated event, such as the title, description, and other relevant details.
